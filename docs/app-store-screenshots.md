## Additional Recommendations

### Device Frames
- Use iPhone 15 Pro and Pixel 8 frames for maximum appeal
- Dark mode recommended for most screenshots (matches app brand)
- Consider notch/Dynamic Island placement when cropping

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, 28-32pt, white or emerald
- Subtext: Regular, 16-18pt, white/70 or muted
- Font: Inter or SF Pro Display for consistency

### Color Palette
- Primary: Emerald (#10b981) for highlights and CTAs
- Background: Dark (#0a0a0a or #1a1a1a)
- Accent: White for text, subtle gradients for depth

### A/B Testing Suggestions
1. Test "AI That Thinks Before You Bet" vs "Your AI Sports Analyst"
2. Test leading with Live Scores vs AI Prediction
3. Test including pricing in screenshot vs clean feature-only

### Localization Notes
- Adapt headlines for markets (UK: "football" not "soccer")
- Consider region-specific sports in screenshots (cricket for India/UK)
- Translate trust messaging carefully—"No Ads" resonates globally
